Muddy Cwtch

Muddy Cwtch provides accessible mountain biking and nutrition weekenders to the South Shropshire Hills - my boyfriend leads the mountain biking and I look after the food side of things.

How it works

A Muddy Cwtch weekender is 2 days of good food, good company and mountain biking - for all levels. The food on a Muddy Cwtch weekender is the fuel for the experience - tasty, satisfying and nutritionally sound.  I've given lots of thought to what breakfast will get riders out of bed and up a hill. What lunch and snacks will keep them energised, focused and sustained. And finally, what food will help them recover and prepare their mind and body for round two (especially after a Shropshire Ale or two).
