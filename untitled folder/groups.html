Groups

Groups are a great way to access nutritional support for you and your friends and family in a purse friendly and ultimately supportive (and fun!) way.

What's involved

Group sessions are delivered in your home in either Shrewsbury (and surrounding areas) or London. Alternatively I also run them from The Centre for Integral Health, Shrewsbury. Workshops last for 2 hours - think Tupperware parties or Anne Summers but without the kinkiness. If you and a group of friends or family (up to 5) are interesting in holding a ‘Something on a plate’ workshop get in touch and I can shape something to fit you and your groups needs. Examples of topics could be achieving optimum health for pregnancy, addressing a diabetes diagnosis in the family or nutritional support for weight loss. This format also works well for small corporate workshops (numbers can vary).

Next steps

For group workshops cost will vary depending on numbers and location - but remember the cost is shared! Contact me and we can discuss what you and your group have in mind.
