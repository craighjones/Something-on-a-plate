Consultations

No need for a stopwatch; your consultation will last longer than 5 minutes and we will discuss all your symptoms. I provide 121 consultations from The Centre for Integral Health in Shrewsbury as well as home visits in London.

What's involved

For individual consultations before you come to your consultation I will get you to complete a confidential questionnaire and 3-day food diary. During the initial session, which lasts 1.5hrs , a thorough case history is taken to build up a detailed picture of your health. You will be provided with a programme which will fit practically into your lifestyle and it is then recommended that you come for a follow up appointment (45 minutes) in around 8 weeks time to assess your progress and make any adjustments. From one to three follow ups are normally required depending on the individual case.

At the end of your time with me you will receive an individualised programme that addresses potential food allergies, dietary imbalances and nutrient deficiencies amongst many other things that may be contributing to your symptoms. Equally important - you will leave having been listened to, with a greater belief in how accessible good nutrition actually is and will feel empowered to make better decisions around the food you (and your family) eat.

Next steps

For individual consultations your first appointment (1.5hrs) will cost £65 and follow up (45mins) £45 contact me and we can discuss things first before agreeing on next steps.
